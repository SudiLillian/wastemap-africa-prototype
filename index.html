Community reports that support better flood planning.

WasteMap Africa brings together waste reports, drainage conditions and exposure data so city teams can identify priority areas and plan a response.

Risk method

Flood Risk Score

Hazard×Drain failure×Exposure×Vulnerability
  • HazardRainfall, terrain, river proximity and historic flooding
  • Drain failureBlockage, remaining capacity and downstream constraints
  • ExposurePeople, roads, schools, clinics and bridges
  • VulnerabilityAccess, housing conditions and response capacity
Why this matters

Waste and flooding are connected.

In many neighbourhoods, drains are blocked by waste or sediment before heavy rain arrives. City teams often receive scattered reports but lack one view of the most urgent locations.

The pilot focuses on turning local reports into a clear list of sites to inspect, clear and monitor.
